Naman Hall

Exquisite facilities merged with impressive traditional bamboo architecture the rectangular shaped, asymmetrical pitched roof conference hall provides a hi-tech yet elegant environment for meetings and social gatherings.

With a simple and luxurious style, the hall will easily adapt to many different business needs while creating a sophisticated and classy vibe. The vault bamboo structure gives the building an iconic and jaw-dropping look that make your event even more memorable.”
