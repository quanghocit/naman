Loyalty Program

It’s always our wish to welcome you back again!

This program is for customers of Naman Retreat with the special privilege for customers who own a membership card. There are 3 card levels based on the number of your returning with corresponding privileges through direct booking via Naman Retreat website or Reservation department of the hotel.

For the 1st returning booking

10% room rate discount from any public rates (or any online rates)

FOC round-trip airport transfer

VIP welcome room amenities

10% discount for all retreat services except for Tours & Golf (not applied with any in-house packages and promotions)

Free 02 pieces of laundry per bedroom per day

For the 2nd returning booking

15% off on any public room rates (or any online rates)

Free of charge round-trip airport transfer

15% off on all F&B services including mini-bar & Spa a-la-carte Menu/Special Treatment

VIP welcome amenities in room

A Credit Voucher (from VND 1.000.000 to VND 3.000.000 depending on room categories) for every 3-night-stay, being redeemed for all services provided in the retreat, except Golf & Tours.

Free of charge 02 pieces of laundry per bedroom per day

For the 3th returning booking and more

20% off on any public room rates (or any online rates)

Free of charge round-trip airport transfer

20% off on all F&B consumption including mini-bar & SPA Ala Carte Menu/Special Treatment

VIP welcome amenities in room

A Credit Voucher (from VND 1.500.000 to VND 5.000.000 depending on room categories) for every 3-night-stay being redeemed on all services provided in Naman Retreat, except Golf & Tours.

Free of charge 02 pieces of laundry per bedroom per day

Above entitlement is applied only for direct booking through Naman Retreat Reservation Department: reservations@namanretreat.com
